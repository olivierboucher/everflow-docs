---
title: Get Invoice
openapi: /openapi/affiliate-invoices.yaml get /affiliates/billings/affiliates/invoices/{invoiceId}
---

Retrieve full details for a single invoice including the line item breakdown and payment history. Line items indicate how the invoice total was calculated, with types including:

- **offer_detail** -- earnings from specific offers
- **referral** -- referral commission earnings
- **vat** -- tax entries
- **adjustment** -- manual adjustments made by the network

The payments array shows all payments applied to the invoice, including the payment method, amount, date, and reference ID.
