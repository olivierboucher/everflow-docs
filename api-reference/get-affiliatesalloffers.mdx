---
title: List Visible Offers
openapi: /openapi/affiliate-offers.yaml get /affiliates/alloffers
---

Browse the full catalog of offers visible to your affiliate account. This includes both public offers and offers that require approval before you can run them. Use this endpoint to discover new offers and check your application status.

The `relationship.offer_affiliate_status` field tells you whether you have been approved, are pending review, or have not yet applied for each offer. To see only offers you are authorized to send traffic to, use the **Find All Runnable Offers** endpoint instead.
