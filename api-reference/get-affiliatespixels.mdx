---
title: List Postbacks
openapi: /openapi/affiliate-postbacks.yaml get /affiliates/pixels
---

List all postbacks (pixels) configured for your affiliate account. Postbacks notify your systems when conversions, events, or clicks occur. Filter results by offer ID, status, type, or delivery method to find specific postbacks.

Use this endpoint for a quick overview of all configured postbacks. For advanced search with pagination and search terms, use the **Search Postbacks** endpoint instead.
