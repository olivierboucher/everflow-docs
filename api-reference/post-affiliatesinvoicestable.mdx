---
title: List Invoices
openapi: /openapi/affiliate-invoices.yaml post /affiliates/billings/affiliates/invoicestable
---

Retrieve a paginated list of invoices for your affiliate account. Supports search filters, sorting, and pagination to help you find and browse invoices programmatically. Filter by payment status (`paid` or `unpaid`), date range, and balance thresholds.

Each invoice includes the billing period, total billed and paid amounts, outstanding balance, payment terms, and currency. Use the **Get Invoice** endpoint to retrieve the full line item breakdown and payment history for a specific invoice.
