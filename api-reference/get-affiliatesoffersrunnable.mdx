---
title: List Runnable Offers
openapi: /openapi/affiliate-offers.yaml get /affiliates/offersrunnable
---

Returns only the offers your affiliate account is authorized to generate traffic for. This includes public offers and offers where your application has been approved. Unlike the **List Visible Offers** endpoint, every offer returned here is one you can actively promote.

The response includes detailed relationship data for each offer:

- **creatives** -- available banners, links, and other promotional materials
- **payouts** -- your payout type and amounts
- **urls** -- destination URLs you can use in tracking links
- **remaining_caps** -- how much cap budget is left for the current interval
- **channels** -- approved traffic channels
