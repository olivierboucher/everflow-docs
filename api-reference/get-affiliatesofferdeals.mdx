---
title: Get Offer Deals
openapi: /openapi/affiliate-deals.yaml get /affiliates/offers/{offerId}/deals
---

Retrieve all deals associated with a specific offer. Deals represent promotional offers such as coupon codes, discounts, and limited-time sales that affiliates can promote to their audience. Each deal includes coupon codes, discount amounts, validity dates, and purchase thresholds.

Use this endpoint when you need deals scoped to a single offer. To fetch deals across multiple offers at once, use the **Search All Deals** endpoint instead.
