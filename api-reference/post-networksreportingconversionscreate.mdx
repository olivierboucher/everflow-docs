---
title: Create Conversion
openapi: /openapi/reporting-extras.yaml post /networks/reporting/conversions
---

Manually fire a conversion. Use this to create conversions that were not captured through normal tracking, such as offline conversions or manual attribution adjustments.

Provide `network_offer_id` and `network_affiliate_id` at minimum. Optionally include `amount`, `transaction_id`, and sub-parameters (`adv1`-`adv5`, `sub1`-`sub5`).
