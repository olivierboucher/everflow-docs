---
title: List Coupon Codes
openapi: /openapi/affiliate-coupon-codes.yaml get /affiliates/couponcodes
---

Retrieve all active coupon codes assigned to your affiliate account. Only returns coupon codes for offers you are authorized to run. Each coupon code includes its tracking link, validity dates, description, and the associated offer details.

Use coupon codes in your promotions to offer discounts to your audience. The `tracking_link` field provides a ready-to-use link that automatically attributes conversions using the coupon code to your affiliate account.
