---
title: List Traffic Controls
openapi: /openapi/affiliate-traffic.yaml get /affiliates/trafficcontrols
---

Retrieve all traffic control rules that apply to your affiliate account. Traffic controls define how your traffic is handled based on variable matching, including blocks, redirects, and other actions. Each rule includes its status, control type, validity dates, and the matching variables.

Use this endpoint to understand what traffic rules are in effect and how they might affect your campaigns. Rules can apply to all offers or specific ones, as indicated by the `is_apply_all_offers` field.
